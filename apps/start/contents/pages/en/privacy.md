## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account data** — email address and name when you sign in.
- **Usage data** — skill installs, search queries, and page views (aggregated).
- **Technical data** — IP address, browser type, and request logs retained for 30 days.

## 2. How We Use It

We use your data to operate the registry, send transactional emails, and improve the platform. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## 3. Cookies

See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details on what cookies we set and why.

## 4. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Aggregated usage data may be retained indefinitely.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@skills.re.
