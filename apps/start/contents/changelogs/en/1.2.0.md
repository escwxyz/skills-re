The evals tab on every skill page now shows the full test suite used to validate the skill's behaviour. This makes it easier to understand what a skill actually does before installing it — and gives authors a clear signal of quality expectations.

Sandbox execution is rate-limited to 5 runs per visitor per day during the preview period.
