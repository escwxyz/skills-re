## Cookies We Set

| Name             | Purpose                                 | Duration |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------- | -------- |
| `session`        | Keeps you signed in                     | Session  |
| `locale`         | Remembers your language preference      | 400 days |
| `theme`          | Remembers light/dark preference         | 400 days |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie preference choice | 365 days |
| `sidebar_state`  | Remembers whether the filters sidebar is open | 7 days   |

## Third-Party Cookies

We do not load third-party analytics or advertising scripts that set cookies.

## Controlling Cookies

You can clear cookies through your browser settings at any time. Removing the `session` cookie will sign you out. Removing `locale`, `theme`, or `sidebar_state` will reset those preferences to defaults.
