---
question: What licenses are supported?
order: 5
---

Any SPDX-recognised license identifier is accepted in the `license` frontmatter field. The most common choices in the registry are:

- **MIT** — permissive, allows commercial use and redistribution
- **Apache-2.0** — permissive with patent grant
- **CC-BY-4.0** — Creative Commons attribution
- **Proprietary** — all rights reserved (use `Proprietary` as the value)

You can also use `UNLICENSED` to explicitly reserve all rights without a formal proprietary notice.

The registry does not enforce license compliance on the consumer side — it is your responsibility to check the license of any skill you install. Skills with `Proprietary` licenses are shown with a prominent notice in the registry.
